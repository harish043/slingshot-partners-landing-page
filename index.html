Use Case: E-commerce Automation

See how we would approach a real-world problem for a mid-sized e-commerce client to drive efficiency and boost sales.

The Challenge

A growing online retailer is struggling with a high volume of repetitive customer support inquiries and is frequently overstocking unpopular products, leading to wasted capital and storage costs.

  • High operational costs from manual support tasks.
  • Poor inventory management leading to financial loss.
  • Missed sales opportunities due to inefficient lead scoring.

The Solution

We implement a two-pronged AI strategy:

  • An AI-powered chatbot to handle 70% of routine customer inquiries (order tracking, returns).
  • A predictive AI model to forecast product demand based on sales data, seasonality, and market trends.

The Results

40%

Reduction in customer support response times.

20%

Decrease in overstocked inventory.

15%

Increase in conversion rates from AI-driven lead scoring.
